WHAT IS CLOAKSEED?
================================================================================
A client-side web app that lets users:
  1. Create a personal 2048-word cipher overlay
  2. Encode their real BIP-39 seed phrase  
  3. Write down only meaningless-looking words
  4. Recover their wallet anytime by pasting the cloak phrase
  
Core Concept: If someone finds your notes, they see random words. Only you can
map them back to your real wallet because only you know the cipher.
